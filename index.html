Behind Halo House

Carly and Hayley are visionary sisters and the family owners of Sorella, known for creating weddings that feel effortlessly elegant and deeply personal. Drawing on years of experience running events on their own property, they have developed a reputation for transforming any space into a breathtaking celebration while flawlessly managing even the most complex details. Celebrated for their distinctive design sensibility and thoughtful approach, they specialize in creating highly curated, boutique weddings that reflect each couple's unique style and story. With a calm, attentive presence, Carly and Hayley are trusted by couples who want their wedding day to feel seamless, inspired, and unforgettable...down to the very last touch.
